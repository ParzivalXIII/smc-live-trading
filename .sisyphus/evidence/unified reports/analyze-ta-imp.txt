PART 2: LATENT BUG FIXED
--------------------------

rsi_label(NaN) — returns "overbought" (was incorrect)
  Root cause: float("nan") < 30 evaluates to False in Python, so NaN RSI
  falls through all zone checks to the final else: "overbought".
  Fix: Added math.isnan(rsi) guard returning "insufficient data".
  Test: 11 parametrized RSI cases including NaN boundary.
